PUMP-HOUSE EXERCISES

DEADLIFT

Deadlifts are highly effective at increasing functional strength due to the activation of your largest lower body muscles.

BENCH PRESS

Bench presses can be used to increase strength, improve endurance, and tone the muscles of the upper body

SHOULDER PRESS

The shoulder press is one of the best exercises for strengthening your shoulders and upper back by working out your deltoids, triceps, trapezius and pecs

BARBELL SQUAT

Squatting is the only compound exercise that targets gluteus maximus, quadriceps,hamstrings ,adductor ,hip flexors,calves effectively

BICEP CURLS

The biceps curl is a highly recognizable weight-training exercise that works the muscles of the upper arm and improves strength and definition

TRICEP PUSHDOWN

The triceps pushdown exercise involves resisting against force to straighten your arms and benefits your overall strength and endurance by engaging your core, back, and shoulders.
